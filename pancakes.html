Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • Butter for cooking

Steps to Prepare

  1. Mix all ingredients to form a smooth batter.
  2. Heat a pan and lightly grease with butter.
  3. Pour batter onto the pan and cook until golden brown on both sides.
  4. Serve with syrup or toppings of your choice.

Food Information

Cooking Time 15 minutes
Servings 2 people
Calories 350 kcal per serving
