Margherita Pizza

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Fresh mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil

Steps to Prepare

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a baking sheet.
  3. Spread tomato sauce over the dough, then top with mozzarella.
  4. Bake the pizza for 10-12 minutes until the cheese is melted.
  5. Garnish with fresh basil leaves and a drizzle of olive oil.

Food Information

Cooking Time 15 minutes
Servings 1 pizza
Calories 700 kcal per pizza
