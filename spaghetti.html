Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps to Prepare

  1. Boil the spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, cook the beef until browned.
  3. Add chopped onion and minced garlic; saut√© until soft.
  4. Pour in canned tomatoes, salt, and pepper; simmer for 15 minutes.
  5. Serve the sauce over spaghetti and enjoy.

Food Information

Cooking Time 30 minutes
Servings 2 people
Calories 450 kcal per serving
