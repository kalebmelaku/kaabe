Omelette

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1/4 cup chopped vegetables (optional)
  • Cheese (optional)

Steps to Prepare

  1. Beat the eggs with salt and pepper in a bowl.
  2. Heat the butter in a pan over medium heat.
  3. Pour in the eggs and cook for 2-3 minutes until set.
  4. Add vegetables or cheese if desired, then fold the omelette and serve.

Food Information

Cooking Time 5 minutes
Servings 1 person
Calories 250 kcal per omelette
