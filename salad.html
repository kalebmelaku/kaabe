Caesar Salad

Ingredients

  • Romaine lettuce
  • Croutons
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Caesar dressing
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps to Prepare

  1. Chop the lettuce into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Add croutons and parmesan cheese.
  3. Drizzle Caesar dressing over the salad.
  4. Toss the salad and serve immediately.

Food Information

Cooking Time 10 minutes
Servings 2 people
Calories 300 kcal per serving
